CA Endevor and DevSecOps: Integrating Mainframe Change Control into Secure CI/CD Pipelines

Author: Yiqi Gu
Published: 2024
Affiliation: IBM Expert Lab, Australia

As DevSecOps becomes the standard for secure and automated software delivery, mainframe environments are evolving to integrate change control tools like CA Endevor with broader CI/CD pipelines.

CA Endevor remains a foundational component in z/OS lifecycle governance. It provides structured versioning, promotion automation, and audit tracking, which aligns directly with the principles of security and compliance embedded in DevSecOps.

In regulated sectors—especially banking and government—where separation of duties and controlled deployment paths are non-negotiable, Endevor helps enforce:

  • Immutable promotion workflows
  • RACF-integrated access and approval layers
  • Exit-based policy enforcement
  • Audit-friendly activity tracking

Through integration with tools like ServiceNow, Jenkins, or REST interfaces, Endevor can be embedded into secure pipelines where each change is validated, traceable, and policy-bound.
